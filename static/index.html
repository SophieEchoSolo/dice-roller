Dice Summary:

The dice that you as a player will be using most are the ability dice(d8). They make up the basis of your dice pool. These dice possess positive symbols, and are opposed by difficulty dice.

Difficulty dice(d8) are the basis of the negative dice pool, and will be used/assigned most by the GM. These dice possess negative symbols that oppose those found on the ability dice.

Next are proficiency dice (d12). These are most often seen when making skill checks using a skill that a character has trained in. These dice possess a higher number of positive pips, and they feature the triump pip.

Proficiency dice are opposed by challenge dice(d12). These dice are usually seen during high difficulty situations such as battling elite or prepared opponents. These dice posses a higher number of negative pips, and also sport the despair pip.

Finally, you have the boost(d6) and setback(d6) dice. These dice are usually added to the pools when small outside factors are present in the situation, such as an ally helping on a roll or by strain distracting your character.

EOTE Dice Roller

This app is used to roll the unique dice from the Edge of the Empire tabletop game. The app is currently under construction(Force die to be added at a later date), but will work as follows:

  1. Build your positive and negative dice pool
  2. Click the "Let's Roll!" button
  3. The app shows you your total roll and the aggregate result

To build your dice pools you will need to:

  1. Determine your dice pool based on your characteristics and your skills
  2. Build the negative dice pool based on your GM's instruction or the stats of the opposing character
  3. Analyze the aggregate result to determine your success/failure, advantage/threat, and any other bonuses

For a more detailed explanation, check out one of the FFG core rulebooks (Edge of the Empire, Age of Rebellion, or Force and Destiny)
